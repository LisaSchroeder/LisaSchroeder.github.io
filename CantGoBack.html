Can't Go Back

I designed this game in the course "Schalt um auf Gestaltung" led by Andrea Dilzer at the HFK Bremen. The goal of the course was to create something by taking an personal topic into account. My topic was "Can't Go Back". So I've created a game in which every player's decision can have both a positive and negative impact on the gameplay. As in real life, you can not undo all decisions. You "Can't Go Back".
