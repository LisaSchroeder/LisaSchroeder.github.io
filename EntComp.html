Video Game Prototype

Our game prototype is a first person action adventure, more precise a dungeon crawler. We just simplified the idea and broke it down to the first level of the entire plan, The main character, which is also the player, got arrested, due to the use of black magic or magic in general. The player starts at the bottom of a huge dungeon and has to find a way out of the crazy maze. The authority, who arrested the protagonist, took away all of his magic coins. These coins are from the leader of the mages and quiet powerful. Only after the player collected all of the coins in one level, he is able to continue to the next ones to find a way out of the jail. The dungeon offers various kinds of dangerous objects, such as enemies and traps. We implemented just one level to demonstrate the main ideas of mechanics, goals and aesthetics of the game. Please keep in mind that this is only a prototype.
